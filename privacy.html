§ 2 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS app PhotoDelete and to this privacy policy website. It explains which data is processed when you use the app and when you access this website.

§ 3 Scope of Data Processing in the App

PhotoDelete is an app for locally cleaning up your photo and video library. The app requires access to your photo library so that you can view, review, sort, keep, favorite, or select photos and videos for deletion.

The app processes in particular the following information locally on your device:

  • previews and displays of your photos and videos
  • creation dates and available media metadata
  • file sizes, where available through Apple’s PhotoKit framework
  • media types such as photo, video, screenshot, or Live Photo
  • swipe decisions, such as keep or delete
  • cleanup progress, for example by month or category
  • app settings, such as sorting options or video sound status
  • information related to free usage, such as swipe counters or cooldown times

This data is generally processed locally on your device. Your photos and videos are not transferred to servers operated by the developer.

§ 4 Legal Bases

The processing of your data is based on the following legal bases:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR, where processing is necessary to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR, where you voluntarily grant an iOS permission, such as access to your photo library.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR, where we have a legitimate interest in secure operation, support, and abuse prevention.

§ 5 Photo Access and iOS Permissions

Photo Access

PhotoDelete requests access to your photo library to display photos and videos, read metadata such as date and file size, and create local cleanup sessions.

Limited Photo Access

iOS allows you to grant PhotoDelete access only to selected photos and videos. In this case, PhotoDelete can only display and process the media you have shared with the app. You can change or revoke access at any time in the iOS system settings.

Deleting and Favoriting

If you choose to delete or favorite media, PhotoDelete uses Apple’s PhotoKit functions. Deletion actions are only triggered after your selection and confirmation. Deleted media is generally moved to the iOS “Recently Deleted” album and may be recoverable there for a limited time, depending on your iOS and iCloud settings.

§ 6 No Transfer of Your Photos and Videos to Us

PhotoDelete does not transfer your photos or videos to the developer and does not operate its own server for processing your media library. Media processing takes place on your device through Apple’s PhotoKit framework.

If you use iCloud Photos, Apple may process your photos and videos according to your iCloud settings. PhotoDelete has no independent access to this data beyond the photo permissions provided by iOS. Apple’s Privacy Policy applies in this respect: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

§ 7 Local Storage

PhotoDelete may store app data locally on your device, such as cleanup sessions, swipe decisions, app settings, progress, cached data, and information related to free usage.

This data remains on your device until you change it through use of the app, delete app data, or uninstall the app. When the app is uninstalled, locally stored app data is generally removed.

PhotoDelete offers optional premium features through in-app purchases and auto-renewable subscriptions. Purchases and payments are processed through Apple’s App Store. The developer does not have access to complete payment data such as credit card numbers.

To manage purchase and subscription status, purchase restoration, and premium access, PhotoDelete uses RevenueCat, Inc., 1100 Alakea St, Honolulu, HI 96813, USA.

RevenueCat may process in particular the following technical information:

  • pseudonymous app user ID or comparable identifier

  • subscription status, renewal status, and expiration dates
  • technical app and device information, where required for purchase management

Your photos and videos are not transferred to RevenueCat. Further information is available in RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/
